My Journey

I'm currently pursuing my Master's in Data Science, building upon a strong foundation in Information Technology from my Bachelor's degree. My journey into data science began during my undergraduate studies when I discovered the power of data-driven decision making in IT projects.

My background in IT has given me a unique perspective on data science, emphasizing not just analysis but also implementation, scalability, and practical applications. I believe that the most impactful data science solutions are those that can be effectively integrated into real-world systems.

My research interests lie at the intersection of machine learning and information systems, particularly in developing scalable solutions for data-intensive applications and improving data governance through automated systems.
